📅 Outlook Calendar
Import your calendar from Outlook as an .ics file. Events will appear as blue cards in your planner for the matching week.
How to export from Outlook:
Web: Settings → View all Outlook settings → Calendar → Shared calendars → export
Desktop: File → Open & Export → Import/Export → Export to iCalendar (.ics)
📊 Team Schedule (Excel)
Upload a new shift schedule Excel file to update the Team Schedule tab. The file must follow the same format as the original.
Note: The schedule is saved in your browser. After uploading, switch to the Team Schedule tab to verify it loaded correctly.
